2021 Summer Service Trip

During the summer 2021 we are taking 30 volunteers, ages 15 and up, to Madagascar to help build a new extension to our school.
Program fees are $1,300 for a private room and $1,200 for a shared room. Fees include in-country transportation, comfortable accommodations, 3 restaurant quality meals a day, bottled water and 2 excursions.
Airfare to Madagascar is not included in the program fee and ranges from $1,000-$2,700 for the period which we will be traveling.
Madagascar does not require any vaccines to travel. It is recommended to be up to date on all vaccines before you travel internationally. You will be asked if you have received the Yellow Fever vaccine in the last 10 years when purchasing your travel visa at the TNR airport upon your arrival. Yellow fever vaccine is required only if you are traveling from an infected country.
We can only accommodate 30 people on the service trip, so make your reservation as soon as possible!
