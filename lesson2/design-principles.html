Design Principle Document

Julien Connault

Repetition

Le Bon Coin

leboncoin.fr

Repetition is used efficiently on Le Bon Coin website, with the repetition of the orange color in the hamburger icon, the page logo/title, the background for the first section and the background for the button. It creates a cohesive design.

Alignment

State of CSS 2020

stateofcss.com

In The State of CSS, alignment is used efficiently with the title and paragraph, having the same padding on the left and right, as well as the "View Results" button below. The design is clean and easy to read.

Fitt's Law

Burger King France

burgerking.fr

With the Burger King France website, we have an interesting demonstration of Fitt's law, where instead of having a ton a tiny dots representing all the restaurants, they grouped the restaurants by regions and added easily clickable icons.
