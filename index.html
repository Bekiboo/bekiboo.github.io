What I do

Project Management

I currently manage two small scale programs in Madagascar. The first with the Elevatus Foundation in which I supervise a children sponsoring and tutoring program, and the second with Bountiful Children Foundation, where we distribute food supplements to malnourished children in 3 cities.

Program Implementation

After successfully implementing the programs for Elevatus and BCF, I am today implementing the Eye Care Connect pilot program for CharityVision. I am in charge of recruitment, finances, adapting the program to the challenging circumstances in Madagascar, and all this during the covid-19 pandemic.

Web Development

Undergoing a bachelor in Web Development in BYU-Idaho with so far a 3.94 GPA. My goal is to offer an affordable solution for small and mid-scale non-profit to develop their web applications, showing an excellent communication and understanding of their needs.

Who I am

Humanitarian project manager - Madagascar

As a social entrepreneur, my primary focus is humanitarian. I developed a deep love for Madagascar while serving a full-time mission here, and then decided to move to the Red Island permanently.

With the support of my wonderful wife, I help developing non-profits in Madagascar and implement their programs with the most effective use of their funds. I am currently entrusted by three non-profits to carry their program in Madagascar, recruit on-site teams, handle finances, and keep constant communication channels with the boards and their donor bases.
