The most dynamic city in Bretagne

Timeline of Rennes

  • 1950s - Rennes grows in importance through rural flight and its modern industrial development, partly automotive. The city develops extensive building plans to accommodate upwards of 200,000 inhabitants
  • 1970 - University of Rennes 1 established
  • 1980s - Rennes becomes one of the main centres in telecommunication and high technology industry
  • 2002 - Rennes becomes the smallest city in the world to have a Metro line
  • 2006 - Les Champs Libres cultural centre opens
  • 2020 - Rennes second metro line opens
